How To Setup Raspberry Pi

What You will need

In addition to the Raspberry Pi board itself, you will need:

  • A Micro USB charger
  • An Ethernet cable
  • A microSD card (minimum 8GB, and cards up to 32GB seem to work fine)

These will also come in handy during the initial setup:

  • A USB keyboard
  • An HDMI cable and monitor

Here is a step by step guide to how to setup a raspberry pi

    Step 1: Download Raspberry Pi OS
    Step 2: Flash micro SD card with Raspberry Pi OS using BalenaEtcher
    Step 3: Insert the sd into the bottom of the raspberry pi and plugin in HDMI, Keyboard, Mouse and power
    Step 4: Follow Guide on the screen
